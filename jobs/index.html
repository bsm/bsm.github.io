Software Engineer, London, United Kingdom

We are a small, extremely passionate and highly effective technology team, based in Shoreditch. We are looking for very talented individuals to work with us on exciting high-profile projects in collaboration with our carefully selected partners with sky-high ambition.

What we are offering:

  • A high-tech field of dreams – we are specialised on non-trival projects no one else can build, constantly pushing the boundaries of technology
  • Invaluable experience in a highly competitive industry with the ability to collaborate with innovative teams – from start ups to industry giants
  • Innovation, diversity, and a broad skill-set experience, including modern system architecture, innovative software & data development techniques, real-time decision and optimisation problems, and emerging opportunities in machine learning and artificial intelligence
  • A competitive remuneration package – depending on qualification and experience

Requirements:

  • Minimum three years of solid, professional experience in at least two out of three of: Go, Ruby and JavaScript
  • Foundation in basic aspects of modern software architecture
  • Ability to produce well-tested, readable, commented, and maintainable code
  • Understanding of agile development mindset & tools
  • Basic operations knowledge with understanding of development, build, deploy cycles
  • Well-developed problem solving skills, including troubleshooting and debugging skills
  • Great communication skills and fluent English – we are working with global partners!

You also should:

  • Be organised, manage own work and meet deadlines in a fast paced and dynamic environment
  • Have knowledge of or interest in other programming languages
  • Be able to travel (infrequently, all expenses paid)
  • Be prepared to work on non-trivial problems and to think outside the box
  • Enjoy collaborating with others, be a constructive team player with the ability to manage and delegate to others and take on responsibility

If you are passionate about your work and feel confident about your skill-set, please send us your CV with a short cover letter to: jobs@blacksquaremedia.com
